About me

I was born in Queens Village in NYC. My parents migrated to this country from Guyana to have a better life and give back to their families. They are my biggest role models for their hard working personality. Growing up in NYC and Queens in specific I was surrounded by the amazing diversity of cultures. I went to elementary school and middle school right in Queens Village. However for my high school education I ventured off to Aviation High School where I learned so much about aviation technology and maintenance, which piqued my curiosity for STEM related activities.

Adil has a unique interest in technology and software in specific. As a 19 year old , Adil is currently enrolled in a City University of New York school located in Queens , Queens College. He is pursuing his undergraduate in computer science and is currently a sophmore. In the midst of him earning his degree, he enrolled in Columbia University Full Stack development coding bootcamp and is currently finishing up to get a headstart on his skills as a developer.
